# 👋 Welcome — AI Engineering Onboarding

Hey, and congrats on graduating! This folder is your **8-week guided path** from
"I just finished college" to "I can build AI agents on this platform."

You don't need to know any of this yet. That's the whole point. We'll go step by
step, in plain language, with a small task every day. By the end you'll understand
**the business, the data platform, and how we build AI agents** — and you'll have
built one yourself.

---

## How this works

- **8 weeks**, one file per week. Each week has **daily tasks** (about 2–4 focused hours/day).
- Every day follows the same simple shape:
  - 🎯 **Goal** — what you'll be able to do by end of day
  - 📖 **Learn** — the idea, explained simply
  - 🛠️ **Do** — a small hands-on task
  - 📂 **Read in our repo** — real files here that show the idea in action
  - ✅ **Check yourself** — 2–3 questions; if you can answer them, move on
- Don't rush. Understanding beats speed. If a day takes two days, that's fine.
- Stuck for more than ~30 min? Write down the question and ask. Asking early is a skill, not a weakness.

> **Golden rule:** you are allowed to not know things. Every senior engineer was
> exactly where you are. Curiosity > pretending.
